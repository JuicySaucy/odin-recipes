Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 tsp Diamond Crystal cosher salt
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 box of Japanase Curry mix

Steps

  1. Cut ingredients into uniform shape
  2. Cook until tender and golden
  3. Cover the indgredients with just enough broth
  4. Add secret ingredients with the broth
  5. Do not forget to skim
  6. Add two cubes of curry roux at a time
  7. Watch out when simmering curry
  8. Make a big batch and store
